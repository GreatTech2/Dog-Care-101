Dog Care 101


This web site was created in order to help anyone who is considering buying or has already bought a dog for the first time and has no past experience in taking care of one. This web site will give advice on how to take care of their new dog, the advice given ranges from what type of dog food should be bought and where it might be found. It will also advise on ways to groom your dog which involves nails, hair, and teeth along with where to find professionals who specialise in this. We will also suggest a wide range of different dog breeds to people who do not yet have a dog but are thinking about getting one. The dog breeds recommended on this site such as the Golden Retriever, Airedale Terrier and the Belgian Tervuren were chosen because thay are the easiest to train and take care of.
