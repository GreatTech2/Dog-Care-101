Dogs for Beginners

Below is a list of dog breeds that are recommended to anyone thinking of getting a dog for the first time. The reason these breeds are suggested due to how easy it is to train them.

The Golden Retriever are extremely intelligent making them great as service dogs for the blind and deft. They are also very friendly, tolerant and great with children which means they make for good family pets. Golden Retrievers learn best through games so make sure training is fun and reword them with treats.



The Berger Picard also called the Picardy Shepherd is said to be the oldest breed of the French sheepdog. This dog breed picks up training easily however can be rather stubborn, so it is important to be patient if you get one. They are also extremely energetic and require plenty of exercise either in the form of walking or playing.

The Airedale Terrier are very Intelligent and outgoing which leads them to having a playful personality. It is not advised people in apartments or small homes get this breed of dog as they require very energetic and need a lot of exercise and are better suited for large gardens with hight fences.

They are full of energy so be sure to give them room to play and roam around. The best way to train this breed is through Positive reinforcement and a rewards system.

Belgian Tervuren are extremely intelligent and athletic making them will suited for a verity of tasks such as herding. They are also very sensitive, so it is important that they get plenty of mental and physical stimulation They also do not like being left home alone for long periods of time.



The French Bulldog has a long history as an affectionate companion dog and make for good family friends and show dogs. They are also well suited for owners living apartment buildings and due to there history as companion they are very easy to train. They are also are prone to obesity so it is important to keep an eye on there weight.
