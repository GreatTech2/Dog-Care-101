Food


There are many different kinds of food that a dog can eat. This page will give advice on what kind to give your dog. First it is important to learn what type of food they were given before you got them usually the best way to do so is to ask the employees of the shelter or pet shop you got them from. It is also important to lean how the food affects your dog specifically for example canned food might make them sick while solid food does not. Treats are also important as it can also help in training them early on. They are also able to eat human food such as meat and carrots, but it is advised not to do it regularly as they will gain weight. It is also important you keep them away from anything with onions and chocolate in them as those can be poisonous to dogs.
